<p>after downloading <a href="http://www.asp.net/sandbox/app_blinq.aspx?tabid=62" rel="tag">blinq</a> and the may linq ctp, i decided to give it a quick run.</p>
<p>first, the installer just kinda installs on you not telling you anything.&nbsp; blinq gets installed into c:\program files\microsoft asp.net\blinq.</p>
<p>if you thought there was a gui, nada (yet).&nbsp; it is command line baby!</p>
<p>so i ensured i had the trusty pubs database installed on my local instance of <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/vstudio/express/sql" rel="tag">sql express 2005</a>.&nbsp; then i went to town.</p>
<p>to scaffold a database, just do the following:</p>
<p>blinq /t:&lt;yourdesiredoutputpath&gt; /server:&lt;yoursever&gt; /database:&lt;yourdb&gt;</p>
