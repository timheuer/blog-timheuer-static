<p>thanks to those who were able to make it to the pre-launch event for <a href="http://halo3.com/believe">halo 3</a> in phoenix!  i hope everyone had a great time being the first in the valley (of the sun) to play the halo 3 game and rock out to some guitar hero.  we had about 55 people for the event plus some staffers.  i know the building access was a pain in the arse, so i appreciated your patience getting in the building.</p>
<p>we gave away 5 xbox 360 elites, 3 copies of halo 3, guitar hero set, halo 3 watches, flash drives, etc.  everyone was able to walk away with at least a halo 3 t-shirt as well!  special thanks to <a href="http://www.mcfarlanetoys.com">mcfarlane toys</a> for donating some items to give away to winners as well!</p>
