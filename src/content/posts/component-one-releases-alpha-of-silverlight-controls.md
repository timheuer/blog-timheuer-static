<p>codenamed <a href="http://labs.componentone.com/Sapphire/">'sapphire</a>,' componentone was one of the first to the market with their plans of <strong>silverlight user controls</strong>.  initially they put a bunch out there on their lab site to play with, but now you can sign up for their alpha version.  that's right, get your hands on some <strong>silverlight controls from componentone</strong>!</p>
<p>now named <a href="http://labs.componentone.com/Sapphire/">Studio Enterprise Silverlight Toolset</a>, they are taking alpha sign-ups.  to me, this is definitely a product you want to look early at.  they've got:</p>
<ul>
    <ul>
        <li>buttons </li>
        <li>containers </li>
        <li>lists </li>
        <li>sliders </li>
        <li>text input </li>
        <li>datagrid </li>
        <li>chart </li>
    </ul>
</ul>
<p>see what i mean?  definitely worth looking at.</p>
