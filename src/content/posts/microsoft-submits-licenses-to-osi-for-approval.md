<p>about two weeks after it was mentioned at oscon, microsoft has submitted 2 licenses to the <a href="http://www.opensource.org">OSI</a> for approval.  you can <a href="http://www.eweek.com/article2/0,1895,2169433,00.asp">read more about it here</a>.</p>
<p>i've previously mentioned i think this is a good thing and the OSI process of license approvals is a public one, allowing for comments and seeing the process vetted in the community, not just behind closed doors.  this will be interesting to follow.</p>
