<p>36 teams.  1 winner.  join the <a href="https://designchallenge.phizzpop.com/">phizzpop design challenge</a>.  from the site:</p>
<p><em>"The PhizzPop Design Challenge pits top interactive, Web, and design agencies against one another to push the </em><em>limits of technology and creativity in a battle royale."</em></p>
<p>if you want to join in the festivities, go register for a location at the <a href="https://designchallenge.phizzpop.com/">phizzpop design challenge</a> site.  the first of these challenges happened earlier this year in san francisco and looked like a great time.  the teams were presented with a challenge use case and given time to come up with a solution.  various implementations in various technologies emerged (ajax, windows presentation foundation, <a rel="tag" href="http://silverlight.net">silverlight</a>, etc.) and a winner was chosen by the design community judges.</p>
<p>design smackdown baby.</p>
