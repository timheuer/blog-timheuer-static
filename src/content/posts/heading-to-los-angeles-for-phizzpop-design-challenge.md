<p><em>"Go back to Jersey, Sonny. This is the City of the Angels and you haven't got any wings." (from LA Confidential)</em></p>
<p>i'm headed to los angeles, ca tomorrow for a week of the <a rel="tag" href="http://designchallenge.phizzpop.com/">phizzpop design challenge</a>.  while last week already yielded some training in some of the expression tools and <a rel="tag" href="http://silverlight.net">silverlight</a>, i'm headed out to do my best to mentor those that are taking advantage of the microsoft working areas.</p>
<p>what is the phizzpop design challenge?</p>
<blockquote>
<p>The PhizzPop Design Challenge pits top interactive, Web, and design agencies against one another to push the limits of technology and creativity in a battle royale. Think Mad Max for design.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>basically teams have been chosen and they will be presented with a problem statement on monday.  this problem statement will include instructions to them of what those team should incorporate into their solution.  the implementation, however, will be up to them.  there is a panel of judges who will listen to the teams present their solutions and how they went about solving this particular problem statement.</p>
<p>i've yet to visit the la office and i'm excited to spend some time out there.  i'm coming off a weekend of, well, let's just say "pain."  i'll be bringing my meds though.  more importantly i'm bringing my new camera rig in hops of talking to some of these teams while they are out there.</p>
<p>if anyone knows any celebrities as well that might be in the la area and are willing to do a cheesy public service announcement for internal videos, let me know...i've got some ideas.  if you know anyone in the cast of Heroes that would be sweet...drop me a line.</p>
<p>well, that's where i'll be this week.  i'm hoping to head down to irvine with <a href="http://blog.pewitt.org">woodyp</a> on wednesday and chill out at the installfest down there.  hope to see some of you there! </p>
