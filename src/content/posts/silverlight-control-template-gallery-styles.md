<p>One of the things I hear a lot about it <strong>wanting well-designed control templates/styles</strong> for developers to use in <a href="http://silverlight.net">Silverlight</a> (and WPF).  As someone with that missing creativity gene, I agree.  While we wait for <a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/scottgu/archive/2008/09/25/silverlight-2-release-candidate-now-available.aspx#6642912">some of these galleries</a> to emerge (and designers to contribute), there is some hope for the rest of us today.</p>  <p>For WPF developers, there has been a commercial resource available (not free) in the templates provided by <a href="http://xamltemplates.net">XAMLTemplates.net</a>.  They provide various styles available for purchase for WPF templates and styles.</p>  <p>There also have been some emerging in the <a href="http://silverlight.net/themes/silverlight/community/gallerydetail.aspx?cat=6">Silverlight.net community gallery</a> (look for Dark Skin for Controls) as well (and I encourage anyone to post theirs to the gallery as well).  One recent one (prepped for Silverlight 2 release) is one of the UK’s user experience consultants, <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/dave">Dave Crawford</a>.  Dave just propped up a blog on MSDN and his first post is his contribution to the Silverlight control template world: <em><strong><a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/dave/archive/2008/10/06/dave-s-glossy-controls-for-silverlight-2-released.aspx">Dave’s Glossy Controls</a></strong></em>.  A preview of the included styles shows some subtle templates to provide a distinctive interface:</p>  <p><img src="http://blogs.msdn.com/blogfiles/dave/WindowsLiveWriter/DavesGlossyControlsforSilverlight2releas_8870/splash_v1.2_3.png" /> </p>  <p>Dave released these under <a href="http://www.opensource.org/licenses/ms-pl.html">Ms-Pl</a> as well and demonstrates in his blog post how easy it is to change some of the colors in his templates as well as how easy they are to use in <strong>Expression Blend</strong>.  Thanks for contributing Dave!</p>  <div style="padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display:
