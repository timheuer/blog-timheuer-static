<p>this week i'm at some windows mobile developer events and it is always fun to be around people who have searched out things you haven't found yet...did that make sense?</p> <p>anyway, here's some dump of some cool windows mobile things i've learned about today...</p> <p><a href="http://www.randyrants.com/2006/08/sharp_mt_pocket.html" rel="tag">phone sharpmt</a> - this is a windows mobile smartphone blogging client tool.  not the greatest UI, but it worked for my blog earlier</p> <p><a href="http://www.iliumsoft.com/site/nw/newsbreak.htm" rel="tag">newsbreak</a> - solid rss reader for windows mobile...moreso than some of the others.</p> <p><a href="http://www.quickmark.com.tw/English/download.html" rel="tag">quickmark</a> - take a picture of a UPC symbol with your smartphone and get product information</p> <p><a href="http://www.smart2go.com/en" rel="tag">smart2go</a> - gps navigation system for your phone/gps adapter -- free open source from nokia</p> <p><a href="http://projectdistributor.net/Releases/Release.aspx?releaseId=410" rel="tag">sms voter</a> -- application to enable voting into your mobile device using SMS messaging</p> <p><a href="http://jgui.net/black/vistaspinterface">vista interface for your phone</a> -- i tried this, but with varied success -- memory hog, can't recommend but looks cool</p> <p><a href="http://vixy.net/" rel="tag">vixy.net</a> -- take a youtube, etc. url, feed it into vixy and get the AVI or other formats and put them on your device...great quality</p> <p>maybe more to come as the week progresses...</p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:f5de4b38-5052-4c1d-8052-0ab8c2399b9d" contenteditable="false" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
