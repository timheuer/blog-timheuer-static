<p>get the <a href="http://www.squeet.com/redirect.aspx?redir=http%3a%2f%2fwww.microsoft.com%2fdownloads%2fdetails.aspx%3ffamilyid%3d22e69ae4-7e40-4807-8a86-b3d36fab68d3%26displaylang%3den&amp;f=230&amp;e=5429455">consolas font pack</a></p>
<p>once you do, you can make it your font in visual studio, as well as in <a href="http://www.hanselman.com/blog/UsingConsolasAsTheWindowsConsoleFont.aspx">command prompt windows</a>.</p>
