<p>over the past 6 months i've received numerous questions whether or not a user group for <strong><a title="SharePoint" target="_blank" href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/sharepoint">sharepoint</a></strong> existed in arizona.  it didn't (there was one but it drizzled away), but i'm happy to announce that some volunteers rallied to start another one.  they have set a date for their first meeting or the <strong>arizona sharepoint professionals group</strong>.</p>
<p>here's the information i have:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Date:</strong>  Thursday, 31 JAN 2008<br />
<strong>Time: </strong>4:30 PM - 6:30 PM<br />
<strong>Locations: </strong><a href="http://www.uat.edu">University of Advancing Technology</a>, 2625 W Baseline Rd, Tempe, AZ</p>
</blockquote>
<p>they are asking people to register (not sure why) so if you plan on attending, please do register for them.  you can click the <a href="http://msevents.microsoft.com/CUI/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=1032365776&amp;Culture=en-US">registration link here</a>.  if you experience problems registering, i'd say just show up :-).  sorry, no web site for further information other than the <a href="http://msevents.microsoft.com/CUI/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=1032365776&amp;Culture=en-US">registration site</a>.  if you do go, encourage them to do 2 things for me (i'll be in denver or i'd go): 1) start an email group instead of a forum and 2) post their events on <a href="http://azgroups.com">azgroups.com</a> so others can see (i've posted this first one).</p>
