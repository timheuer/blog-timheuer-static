<p>well, i've been telling my friends and family (secretly replacing their search defaults :-)) that live.com is the bomb and it has been producing more relevant searches for me for the past few months since i told myself i'd start using it.  that's a claim i stand by -- so flame away.</p> <p>until today.  i was responding to a blog post where someone referred to "Fuslogvwr" and i had no idea what that was.  so i <a href="http://search.live.com/results.aspx?q=fuslogvwr">searched on live</a>...no results.  put quotes around it, no results.  and not "no results" in the sense of no relevant results, there were literally none returned.</p> <p>argh, i <a href="http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&amp;lr=&amp;q=fuslogvwr&amp;btnG=Search">used google</a>.  53 results, an a suggestion for 'fuslogvw' (which was the actual exe name).  what a difference.  i went back to live and <a href="http://search.live.com/results.aspx?q=fuslogvw">entered fuslogvw</a> and sure enough, relevant results.</p> <p>and yes, i did report the feedback ;-)</p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:8ccf9b26-9fc1-4be5-9f3f-f64217a1fce7" contenteditable="false" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
