<p>i&rsquo;m a huge fan of the universal remote control&hellip;but not the ones that you point at eachother and &ldquo;learn&rdquo; the codes&hellip;i&rsquo;m talking programmable.&nbsp; it used to be that hard-core home theater fanatics were the only ones that could get them, and they were RF controlled and had to be programmed by the pros.</p>
<p>enter logitech.</p>
<p>they have a line of harmony remote controls that are programmed via the web/usb &mdash; very cool.&nbsp; you just enter your model number(s) of your device(s) and it configures the remote.&nbsp; you set certain activities (macros) so all you do is click one button &lsquo;watch dvd&rsquo; and your tv turns on to the right input, the receiver turns on, and the dvd player turns on, etc.&nbsp; it is very slick.&nbsp; get a new device, just plug it back in and update.</p>
<p>i&rsquo;ve had the <a href="http://www.logitech.com/index.cfm/products/detailsharmony/US/EN,CRID=2084,CONTENTID=8865">harmony 659</a> for a while now and it has been great and reliable.&nbsp; recently i started building out my home theater &lsquo;stuff&rsquo; and acquired a new tv and xbox 360.&nbsp; I figured with the xbox i&rsquo;d use the <a href="http://www.logitech.com/index.cfm/products/detailsharmony/US/EN,CRID=2084,CONTENTID=11250">harmony remote for xbox 360</a> and got it.&nbsp; it sucks.&nbsp; it looks cool, but sucks.&nbsp; it doesn&rsquo;t control my cable box well for some reason and the numbers to change channels are just way too small.&nbsp; i&rsquo;ve not been happy with it.&nbsp; but i was too lazy to return it in time, so i was stuck with it.</p>
<p>i finally couldn&rsquo;t take it anymore, and went out and got the <a href="http://www.logitech.com/index.cfm/products/detailsharmony/US/EN,CRID=2084,CONTENTID=9933">harmony 880</a>.</p>
<p><img alt="" src="http://www.logitech.com/lang/images/0/6615.jpg" border="0" /></p>
<p>sweet!&nbsp; i updated everything (i wish logitech would change their site so i could have one login and choose the remote i want to configure, but i guess they figure not too many people will have many universal remotes <img src="http://www.timheuer.com/images/smile1.gif" />).&nbsp; at any rate, it updated quickly and controlled everything beautifully &mdash; even the cable box.&nbsp; i&rsquo;m as happy as a kid in a candy store.&nbsp; it&rsquo;s even rechargeable so i don&rsquo;t have to worry about batteries.</p>
<p>yeah, i thought about the <a href="http://www.logitech.com/index.cfm/products/detailsharmony/US/EN,CRID=2084,CONTENTID=10930">890</a> for the added bonus of RF control, but i couldn&rsquo;t justify the extra dough for that feature alone &mdash; i don&rsquo;t have any walls to penetrate, etc.</p>
<p>so, if you have a stack of remotes, stop whining and get yourself to a store that sells the harmony from logitech&hellip;you&rsquo;ll be glad you did!</p>
