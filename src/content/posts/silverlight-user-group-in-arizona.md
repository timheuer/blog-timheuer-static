<p><a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/palermo4">mike palermo</a> has decided to start a special interest user group around <a rel="tag" href="http://silverlight.net">silverlight</a>!  the first meeting in the arizona area will be at <a rel="tag" href="http://interfacett.com">interface technical training</a> on 07-NOV at 6:00 PM.  attendance and participation are both free!</p>
<p>please feel free to invite your friends, colleagues, etc and spread the word.  for the first meeting it will be a bit ad-hoc, but i know i'll be there and have volunteered to talk about creating controls, etc.  what do you want to hear about?</p>
<p>you can view the other user group schedules as well as get the address for this meeting by visiting the calendar at <a href="http://www.azgroups.com">www.azgroups.com</a>.  we hope to see you in november!</p>
