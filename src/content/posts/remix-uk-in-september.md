<p />  <p>Well, I wish I was going to <a href="http://www.microsoft.com/uk/remix08">this event: ReMIX UK</a>!  For one, it would be starting the day of my birthday and would be a great birthday treat!  Secondly, I would be able to meet up with my fellow <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/archive/2008/06/06/mix-essentials-mexico-recap.aspx">escamoles chaps</a> and maybe take in some local cuisine instead.</p>  <p>If you are in the UK, or for some reason really wanted to see the current value of the US dollar, make sure you head to <a href="http://www.microsoft.com/uk/remix08/">ReMIX UK</a>!  The lineup is pretty incredible when you look at it…here’s some Microsoft favorites:</p>  <ul>   <ul>     <li><a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/scottgu">Scott Guthrie</a> – I mean, duh…this would make it a no-miss.</li>      <li><a href="http://mtaulty.com">Mike Taulty</a></li>      <li><a href="http://ux.artu.tv">Arturo Toledo</a></li>      <li><a href="http://www.roodyn.com">Dr. Neil Roodyn</a></li>   </ul> </ul>  <p>I also saw on the <a href="http://www.microsoft.com/uk/remix08/agenda.aspx">agenda for the 2 day event</a> that <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/tims">Tim Sneath</a> and <a href="http://silverlight.net/blogs/jesseliberty">Jesse Liberty</a> were listed, so it should be even richer (pun intented) content.  In addition it looks like some great stuff on the designer others!</p>  <p>By the way, I listed Dr. Neil above as one of my Microsoft favorites.  He’s actually one of our Regional Directors.  If you have never met Dr. Neil…do so.  He’s a great guy, very
