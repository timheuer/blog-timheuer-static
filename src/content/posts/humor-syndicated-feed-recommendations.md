<p>I subscribe to a lot of feeds.  I read a lot of them, and a lot I skim.  But I also make sure I subscribe to things that break up my day.  Here’s some of my favorites if you haven’t seen them before:</p>
<ul>
    <li><a href="http://failblog.org">FAILblog.org</a> – the classic.  All things wrong.</li>
    <li><a href="http://awkwardfamilyphotos.com">AwkwardFamilyPhotos.com</a> – stop right now and subscribe.  When I’m feeling down I go here.  Seriously.  I’ve also had a photo of my sister I’ve been threatening to post there.</li>
    <li><a href="http://ugliesttattoos.com">UgliestTattoos.com</a> (warning some NSFW) – I’m am seriously amazed what people put on their bodies.  I’ve got tattoos…but I’m mean…<a href="http://ugliesttattoos.com/2009/08/24/funny-tattoos-beauty-fades-dumb-is-forever/">for real</a>?</li>
    <li><a href="http://photoshopdisasters.blogspot.com">Photoshop Disasters</a> – no graphic advertising intern can sneak crap past these guys.</li>
    <li><a href="http://www.thereifixedit.com">There I Fixed It</a> – amazing how resourceful people are ;-)</li>
</ul>
<p>Just a little bit of syndicated humor does wonders.</p>
