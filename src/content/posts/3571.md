<P>more details: <A href="http://www.azgroups.com/Page.Events.Event-25.aspx">aug 16 meeting</A></P>
<P>join us at the next az .net user group meeting on august 16, presented by community member Lorin Thwaits:</P>
<BLOCKQUOTE>Using the features of GDI+, it is easy to generate GIF, PNG, and JPG images on-the-fly in ASP.NET.&nbsp; The first part of the meeting will showcase simple charts and other graphics rendered with the standard web-safe color palette.&nbsp; We will examine strengths and limitations of the various available image formats.&nbsp; In the second half, we get down and dirty with the raw bitmap data itself for more advanced rendering.&nbsp; C# is used throughout.&nbsp; Come on out and let's have a great time changing the world, one image at a time!</BLOCKQUOTE>
