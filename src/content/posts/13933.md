<p>each year, my wife and i hold a friends/neighborhood party to celebrate the holiday season (yes, the holiday season that stresses me out like you wouldn't believe).</p> <p>at any rate, this year we held our 3rd annual festivus party.  what's festivus?  well, if you are a true seinfeld fan, you'd know.  in one episode (episode "the strike") of seinfeld here was the exchange:</p> <blockquote> <p><b>Frank Costanza:</b> <i>Many Christmases ago, I went to buy a doll for my son. I reached for the last one they had, but so did another man. As I rained blows upon him, I realized there had to be another way.</i></p> <p><b>Cosmo Kramer:</b> <i>What happened to the doll?</i></p> <p><b>Frank Costanza:</b> <i>It was destroyed. But out of that a new holiday was born … a Festivus for the rest of us!</i></p> <p><b>Cosmo Kramer:</b> <i>That must've been some kind of doll.</i></p> <p><b>Frank Costanza:</b> <i>She was.</i></p></blockquote> <p>and thus, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Festivus" rel="tag">festivus</a> was born.  originally celebrated on december 23, my wife and i hold it on the first saturday in december to try to beat the rush of company parties, etc.  we also make it a chili cook-off contest and white elephant raffle shindig as well.  for the past three years, i've dawned my same kelly green pants, and this year added a red blazer (courtesy of ebay of course).  </p> <p><img alt="Festivus III at the Heur's" src="http://static.flickr.com/107/314367166_4265212d0f.jpg" border="0" /></p> <p>others came dressed ala cousin eddie from the christmas vacation movie -- it was awesome.  we had a great time this year and hopefully those that came did as well...it's a good chance to have some fun with friends and neighbors, i highly recommend it.</p> <p>this year we also made party shirts for everyone, here was the design (front/back):</p> <p><img alt="fstv20061front" src="http://static.flickr.com/110/313667295_53f8a6bb7d.jpg" border="0" /> <img alt="FestivusShirt" src="http://static.flickr.com/114/313667274_7647f466e1_m.jpg" border="0" /></p> <p>the back if you can't tell is a bunch of 'holiday' trees with a single <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Festivus#The_Festivus_Pole">aluminum pole</a> in the middle -- commemorating festivus.  why a pole?  well:</p> <blockquote> <p><i>No, instead, there's a pole. It requires no decoration. I find tinsel distracting.<br />-Frank Costanza</i></p></blockquote> <p></p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:ba8fd057-6620-44d2-a4e3-7f24eddc0dac" contenteditable="false" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
