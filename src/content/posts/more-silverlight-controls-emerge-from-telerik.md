<p>this time from <strong>telerik</strong>.  telerik has just announced their <a href="http://www.telerik.com/silverlight">RAD Controls for Silverlight 1.1(2.0)</a>.  they have a site up with some demonstrations, etc. (obviously requiring <strong><a rel="tag" href="http://silverlight.net/">silverlight</a></strong> 1.1 to render).  they have controls like:</p>
<ul>
    <ul>
        <li>upload </li>
        <li>button </li>
        <li>combobox </li>
        <li>listbox </li>
        <li>menus </li>
        <li>progress bar </li>
        <li>tab strip </li>
        <li>textbox </li>
        <li>treeview </li>
        <li>slider </li>
        <li>and some animation controls</li>
    </ul>
</ul>
<p>a pretty comprehensive list.  very cool.  i've always been impressed with the partner ecosystem of microsoft 3rd party developers.  telerik is no substitute.  i knew that they'd be one that would be providing a great control story for silverlight.</p>
<p>take a look at their <a href="http://www.telerik.com/silverlight">demo site</a> and play around.</p>
