<p>i've been trying to learn more and more about <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/wpfe" rel="tag">wpf/e</a> and the current functionality in the february ctp, so i decided to jump on the create-a-video-player-skin bandwagon and mock something up.</p> <p>i thought it would be quite the incredible thing (read: couldn't think of a better idea) to show a <a href="http://www.zune.net" rel="tag">zune</a> player...so whoala:</p> <p><a title="sshot-23" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/66831573@N00/453131314/"><img alt="sshot-23" src="http://static.flickr.com/167/453131314_d94e264d95.jpg" border="0" /></a></p> <p>and when you click 'start player' (yes i know it would be cool to show the player as a playlist and use the normal zune buttons, but c'mon, v.0.0.3 here...work with me people) it rotates to show you the video (if you don't own a zune, the player shows videos landscape rather than portrait).</p> <p><a title="sshot-24" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/66831573@N00/453149403/"><img alt="sshot-24" src="http://static.flickr.com/243/453149403_43cacd07e3.jpg" border="0" /></a></p> <p>anyhow, i'm hoping to evolve this a bit more when i have some more free time -- there is a small bug in the stream downloading calculations that i'm hunting right now.  in case you haven't seen the default video playing, it is the red vs. blue of <a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/scottgu" rel="tag">scott guthrie</a>.  <a href="http://timheuer.com/wpfe/zuneplayer">here's the link to my zune miniplayer</a>.</p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:6c3db60f-09c7-49e4-bebd-5b68dcd78f62" contenteditable="false" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; float: none; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
