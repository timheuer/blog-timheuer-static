<p>In NYC this weekend and want to hang out and learn some <a href="http://silverlight.net">Silverlight</a>?  Some community leaders and Microsoft folk are putting on a <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/peterlau/archive/2008/08/25/silverlight-2-fire-starter-coming-to-nyc-september-13th.aspx">Silverlight 2 Firestarter event this weekend on 13-SEP</a>.  Shawn Wildermuth of the <a href="http://www.silverlight-tour.com">Silverlight Tour</a> will be there for the keynote and giving some sessions as well.  It looks like some folks from Infragistics will be there as well…I’d imagine they might have some cool things to talk about!</p>  <p>The event is free for anyone to attend and should be a good time hanging out with fellow geeks in NYC and learning what others are doing with Silverlight 2!  Registration will probably fill up quick!</p>  <div style="padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; float: none; padding-top: 0px" id="scid:0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:d7347d74-396e-433d-9273-d2e91d3f136e" class="wlWriterEditableSmartContent"></div>
