<p>there's been some questions to me and in my local community about my vague references to my geocoder provider (sorry andrew).  the <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/archive/2006/02/22/7630.aspx">demonstration/explanation of that provider is here in my "geocode provider" post</a> (not sure why i didn't make that part of the code gallery originally, apologies -- it now is).  i hope that helps.</p> <p>i've also just uploaded an example of how you can use virtual earth for geocoding...i've wrapped it in a windows forms app to show that you can leverage virtual earth in a windows forms application as well.  i hope that helps.</p> <p>basically what happens is the form calls a script:</p> <div class="csharpcode"><pre><span class="lnum">   1:  </span><span class="kwrd">private</span> <span class="kwrd">void</span> button1_Click(<span class="kwrd">object</span> sender, EventArgs e)</pre><pre><span class="lnum">   2:  </span>{</pre><pre><span class="lnum">   3:  </span>    AddPushpin.Visible = <span class="kwrd">false</span>;</pre><pre><span class="lnum">   4:  </span>    executeScript(ClearAllPushpins);</pre><pre><span class="lnum">   5:  </span>    executeScript(FindLocation, LocationToFind.Text);</pre><pre><span class="lnum">   6:  </span>}</pre></div>
