<p>Just a quick shout-out to congratulate the latest <a href="http://silverlight.net"><strong>Silverlight</strong></a> MVPs to the program.  The <strong>Microsoft MVP</strong> (Most Valuable Professional) program is a recognition program that is in place to recognize and reward those individuals who have been identified by individuals (peers, Microsoft staff, etc.) as experts in their technology field and global contributors to the technology.  </p>
<p><a href="https://mvp.support.microsoft.com/communities/mvp.aspx?product=1&amp;competency=Silverlight"><img border="0" src="http://storage.timheuer.com/mvp-h-small.png" alt="Microsoft MVP logo" title="Microsoft MVP logo" style="border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></a></p>

<p>All of these folks are great participants in the Silverlight ecosystem and their local geographies and I congratulate them for their efforts.  Be sure to <a href="https://mvp.support.microsoft.com/communities/mvp.aspx?product=1&amp;competency=Silverlight">check out all the Silverlight MVPs</a> and subscribe to their blogs!  Thanks to all of you for helping make a great developer ecosystem!</p>
