<p>so it's not really #137, but it sounded better than #1 and i didn't want to set myself up for a complete list, so my mental brain told me pick a number...(it's been a long morning)</p> <p>a while back i read <a href="http://www.designthinkingdigest.com/">chris</a>' travel tips/realizations (<a href="http://chrisbernard.blogs.com/design_thinking_digest/2007/03/things_that_hap.html">here</a> and <a href="http://chrisbernard.blogs.com/design_thinking_digest/2007/03/things_that_hap_1.html">here</a>)...a few of which i've adopted.  while on another trip with <a href="http://blog.pewitt.org" rel="tag">woodyp</a> a while back, we were chatting about hotels and such and i had been woken up earlier than desired because of the light coming in through the window.  i was commenting on how no matter how much i try to close the drapes, i never seem to be able to get that one little sliver of light to disappear (you should know that i prefer total darkness for bedrooms...i've tried to convince my wife to black out our windows, but she hasn't gone for it yet).</p> <p>woody told me about one of his tricks.  he reminded me that most hotels (unless you are staying at the homestead suites in redmond, which i recommend you don't) have closets that have pants hangers -- you know the ones with the clips.</p> <p>you see where i'm going don't you...</p> <p>woody's tip was using those clip hangars as, well, clips and hold the drapes shut -- maybe one will work, but two definitely.  i'm proud to report i had a chance to use this tip this weekend in portland.  boom.</p> <p>thanks <a href="http://blog.pewitt.org">woody</a>. (and <a href="http://www.designthinkingdigest.com/">chris</a>)</p> <p>oh, and my new favorite travel snack?  <a href="http://www.quakersnackbars.com/CHW_Products/90Calorie/CS_CinnamonSugar.cfm">Quaker Chewy Lowfat Cinnamon Sugar granola bar</a> -- yummy -- and only 90 calories (i guess that means i can eat ten of them?)</p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:36cc026c-06bd-491e-bdec-9551da25f6d9" contenteditable="false" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; float: none; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
