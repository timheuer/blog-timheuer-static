<p>One of my colleagues, <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/corrinab/">Corrina</a>, pointed me today to some research her team is conducting on user experience with Visual Studio and <a href="http://silverlight.net"><strong>Silverlight</strong></a>.  From <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/corrinab/archive/2009/08/30/9889051.aspx">her post today</a>,</p>  <blockquote>   <p>…that my co-workers on the Visual Studio User Experience Team are doing focused on professional and non-professional web/application developers. The research involves… </p>    <ul>     <li>A short survey </li>      <li>A telephone interview to discuss your development experiences </li>   </ul>    <p>A gratuity will be offered to individuals selected to participate in the telephone interview, and selection for interviews is based on responses to the survey. The gratuity is either a $25 Visa/American Express gift card or select Microsoft software titles.</p> </blockquote>  <p>If you are a professional or non-professional web/application developer and would like to be involved in this research, please check out <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/corrinab/archive/2009/08/30/9889051.aspx">her post for more information at the top</a>.</p>  <p>Also Corrina is the one who has been creating the <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/archive/2009/08/13/silverlight-application-themes-expression-gallery-part-2.aspx">application template themes I’ve posted about</a>.  We’ve been talking more about how people use themes that we provide as well as the <a href="http://silverlight.codeplex.com">Silverlight Toolkit</a> themes.  Corrina’s looking for some insight as well:</p>  <blockquote>   <p>One last thing, I have a few theme questions that I would appreciate your feedback on...</p>    <ul>     <li>Do these themes help you get started building your applications (act as inspiration, learning tools...), and, if not, why not? </li>      <li>Do you use these theme's 'as is' with little modification when building your applications? </li>      <li>What type of themes would you like to see in the future (what would help you be more productive when building your applications)? </li>   </ul> </blockquote>  <p>Check out her post and participate in the feedback if you can!</p>  <div style="padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; float: none; padding-top: 0px" id="scid:0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:611eaf9b-1fdf-47ef-bab6-1c3270b2863e" class="wlWriterEditableSmartContent"></div>
