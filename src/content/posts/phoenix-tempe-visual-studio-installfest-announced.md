<p>of course if you are already a part of the broader .net user group community, you've seen this already.  but if you aren't (shame on you), you should head on over to <a href="http://www.azgroups.com">azgroups.com</a> and look at the event on the 20th of December.  be sure to sign up to the <a title="AZGroups" target="_blank" href="http://www.azgroups.com">AZGroups</a> mailing list as well for updates and future events.</p>
<p>at the phoenix (tempe) installfest, people who are <strong>registered and attend</strong> will receive a special trial DVD of visual studio that will enable them to get a <strong>visual studio 2008 professional</strong> licensed copy sent to them as participating in this event...pretty sweet for being a supporter of the user group communities!</p>
