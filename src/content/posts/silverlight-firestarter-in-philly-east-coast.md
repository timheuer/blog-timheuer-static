<p>looks like the flame is making its way to the other side of the coast...the east coast.  looks like in a week (15 DEC) the <strong>silverlight firestarter</strong> will be happening in the philly area (malvern to be specific).  you can <a href="http://smallandmighty.net/2007/11/26/SilverlightFirestarter.aspx">find the agenda here</a> at <a href="http://smallandmighty.net/">dani's blog</a>.</p>
<p>it looks like a great line-up of speakers as well.  although i've not met <a href="http://www.irritatedvowel.com/">pete brown</a> in person, i've 'conversed' with him via e-mail and he's a sharp dude.  <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/peterlau">peter laudati</a> -- well, if you are in the northeast, a microsoft developer, and don't know peter...what's wrong with you? ;-).  and then there is <a href="http://www.smallandmighty.net/">dani</a>.  don't stand too close or his energy might be infectious.  seriously, he's a fun guy to be around.</p>
<p>the topics are spot on for a well-rounded discussion of development of interactive web sites using <a rel="tag" href="http://silverlight.net">silverlight</a> with topics on <a href="http://www.microsoft.com/expression"><strong>expression</strong></a>, core <strong>xaml</strong>, media, and the future of silverlight.  looks like some giveaways will be provided as well!</p>
<p>what a great opportunity for a day of <strong>silverlight</strong> in that area.  be sure to spread the word about it and make sure you register.  since it is the east coast (and on the day of my wife's birthday) i won't be there, but hopefully someone can live blog so i can be there in spirit and see how it is going.</p>
