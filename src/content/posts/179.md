<p>ever wonder how to change the “new” icon duration in sps?  well, in sps2003, there is this command:</p>
<p><span class="code">stsadm.exe -o setproperty -propertyname days-to-show-new-icon -propertyvalue &lt;your-day-desire&gt;</span></p>
