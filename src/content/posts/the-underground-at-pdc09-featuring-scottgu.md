<p><a href="http://www.microsoftpdc.com">PDC</a> is coming…it’s only a few weeks away.  Are you going?  I am and I couldn’t be more excited.  I’m coming in Sunday and helping with a <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/shows/The+Knowledge+Chamber/PDC09-Workshop-Getting-the-Most-out-of-Microsoft-Silverlight-3/">workshop on Monday</a>.  Then the rest of the week will likely be a blur.</p>
<p>If you are going (and even if you aren’t), there is one party you will not want to miss.  Last year "<em>The Underground @ PDC”</em> was a great party and gathering of geeks.  The Gu, <a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/scottgu">Scott Guthrie</a>, was there.  As was Don Box and <a href="http://hanselman.com/">Scott Hanselman</a> dishing out the geek humor and flames toward one another.  It was great.  And the venue for the after-geekiness was amazing…one of the coolest pubs/bar/whatever I’ve been to.</p>
<p>Well, it’s back.</p>
<p align="center"><a href="http://www.undergroundatpdc.com/"><img border="0" style="border: 0px none ;" alt="Underground at PDC logo" src="http://undergroundatpdc.com/_assets/header.jpg" /></a></p>
<p>This year it’s at a place called the Conga Room, which looks to be a latin fusion kind of place.  If there is a Buena Vista Social Club-like band or any form of Mariachi, you might not be able to pull me away.  </p>
<p>The Gu is on the agenda as well for this party.  The best part about this party: FREE.  The only catch – you need an invitation code.  I’ve got one at the bottom of this post.</p>
<p>This event/party is being provided by one of the evangelism teams at Microsoft.  Last year I had a great time mingling with folks and seeing a bunch of live podcast interviews going on with some of the names you’d recognize in the geek world.  It was a blast.  This year promises to be no different.  </p>
<p>You don’t have to be a PDC attendee to go to the party…if you are a SoCal developer and couldn’t make it to PDC, this is for you – you simply have to register.  If you can make it (please don’t register ‘just because’) – <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">visit the </span><a style="text-decoration: line-through;" href="http://undergroundatpdc.com/eventReg.aspx">registration site</a><span style="text-decoration: line-through;"> and use the RSVP code: </span><strong style="text-decoration: line-through;">arpile</strong><span style="text-decoration: line-through;">.  This code is good for a limited amount of registrations…once it’s gone, it’s gone.</span>  This code is at it's maximum registrations.  Follow <a href="http://twitter.com/UndergroundPDC">UndergroundPDC</a> on Twitter where they will be giving out more codes.  I’ve heard about a very cool special surprise that will be there too – can’t say anything though – I’ll be uninvited…and I don’t want that.</p>
<p>I hope to see you at PDC and at <strong>The Underground @ PDC</strong>!</p>
