<P>need to know the name of a song, but don't know who sings it or anything else?</P>
<P>when you hear it, call 866-411-SONG, hold your cell phone up to the speaker...and wait for your SMS message with the title/artist.</P>
<P>i tried it twice with Psycho Killer from the Talking Heads and Cowboy Song from Thin Lizzy and it worked both times.</P>
<P>of course, my cell phone is now strewn across the dialers of the world i'm sure, but still...pretty cool.</P>
