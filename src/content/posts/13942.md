<p>today over at the <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/xna/" rel="tag">xna team blog</a>, michael klucher announced the release of the xna game studio express.  this is a free tool to aid in the development of pc and xbox360 game development.</p> <p>yes, xbox360.  .net + xbox360.</p> <p>how you say?  well, although the xna game studio express tools are free, the ability to deploy them to the xbo360 will require membership in the xna creator's club.  <em>oh sure, wha'ts that a million dollar fee?</em> actually no.  it's $99 US a year.  pretty reasonable i would say.  this helps you get it on the xbox and in the creator's club list on xbox live.  i think it is a pretty cool concept and may even try a few myself.</p> <p>to purchase the xna creators club membership, you go through xbox live marketplace -- <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/xna/archive/2006/12/11/xna-game-studio-express-has-been-released.aspx">read michael's post for details</a>.</p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:dcd042a3-e6be-44af-b8bc-fc535d417bbf" contenteditable="false" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
