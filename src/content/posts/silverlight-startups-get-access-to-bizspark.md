<p>Are you a startup organization?  Perhaps your the next greatest <a href="http://silverlight.net/">Silverlight</a> control vendor or have a great app?  I want to help.  If you’re a Silverlight startup (or any startup really for that matter), reach out to me.  If you haven’t heard of <strong><a href="http://bizspark.com">BizSpark</a> </strong>yet, you’re potentially missing out.</p>  <p>If you meet the following criteria:</p>  <ul>   <li>privately held company </li>    <li>in business less than 3 years </li>    <li>generate less than USD $1 million in revenue </li> </ul>  <p>you may qualify to get access to production licenses for development tools, platform products and server licenses for immediate use.  Take advantage of this and the <a href="http://microsoft.com/web"><strong>Web Platform Installer</strong></a> to jumpstart your biz.  Read the details about BizSpark here: <strong><a href="http://www.microsoftstartupzone.com/BizSpark/Pages/At_a_Glance.aspx">How BizSpark Works</a></strong>.</p>  <p>I’m one of the BizSpark Champs and want to help our startups out there – <em>no matter what you are doing</em> – be successful quickly.  Obviously I’d <em>love</em> to see some Silverlight/RIA goodness, so if you’re in that camp, be sure to let me know!  You can reach out to me via my <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/contact.aspx">contact information</a> to start a dialogue.</p>  <div style="padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; float: none; padding-top: 0px" id="scid:0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:11d5a96c-836b-4b12-83a1-b7c3aad3d30e" class="wlWriterEditableSmartContent"></div>
