<p>looks like the <a href="http://www.silverlight-tour.com/freesignup.aspx">silverlight tour is giving a free ride</a> for a half-day session on <strong><a href="http://silverlight.net/">silverlight</a></strong> in dallas.  i've sat in on one of the early silverlight tour stops before silverlight even was officially released.  i can only imagine that it has gotten more exciting as time goes by.</p>
<p>the dallas event is limited to 32 attendees, so <a href="http://www.silverlight-tour.com/freesignup.aspx">register asap</a> if you can make it.  this free session looks to discuss:</p>
<ul>
    <ul>
        <li>Why should I care about Silverlight </li>
        <li>What is Silverlight? </li>
        <li>When should I use Silverlight? </li>
        <li>What is the Silverlight story on the Server? </li>
        <li>How do developers and designers work together?</li>
    </ul>
</ul>
<p>it will be on 18 FEB in the dallas, tx area (las colinas microsoft campus -- details on the registration link).  register if you can make it and get your free ride :-).</p>
