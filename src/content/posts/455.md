<P>with all the different options available for source control, i began looking at some of the more public available libraries.&nbsp; i've used sourceoffsite and vault and liked them both...but the problem is that the price is a barrier for entry for a small business (yes i know the single user license is free but that doesn't help a team).</P>
<P>so i looked at a few blogs [can't remember them now will update later] and came across the <A href="http://www.nesterovsky-bros.com/html/css2/index.html">SCCBridge</A> project and started taking a look.&nbsp; this provider implements a web service and can utilize your existing ms-vss repository...allowing local users to keep doing what they are doing as well as enabling remote users such as subcontractors, etc.</P>
<P>i encourage you to take a look.&nbsp; because the setup was a bit dispersed, i've included a step-by-step of what i did to get it working and have included all required download links for you to make sure you have everything.&nbsp; check out my <A href="http://timheuer.com/blog/articles/456.aspx">step-by-step process for the SCCBridge setup</A>.</P>
