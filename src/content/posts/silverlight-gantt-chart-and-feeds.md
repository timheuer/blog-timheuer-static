<p>If you want some great <a href="http://silverlight.net">Silverlight</a> information, be sure to subscribe to at least two feeds: <a href="http://silverlight.net/blogs/microsoft/rss.aspx">Microsoft Silverlight Bloggers</a> and <a href="http://silverlight.net/blogs/community/rss.aspx">Silverlight community feeds</a>.  I’ve recently just added a bunch of great feeds to both of these including all the members of the <a href="http://codeplex.com/Silverlight">Silverlight Toolkit</a> team and some Silverlight MVPs such as John Papa.</p>  <p>There are also a bunch of resources being added to the <a href="http://silverlight.net">Silverlight community site</a> over the next month.  We also added <strong>27 new showcase</strong> entries to the <a href="http://silverlight.net/showcase">Silverlight Showcase</a> on the site.  One in particular that I think is interesting is a <strong><a href="http://svdemo02.ilog.com/SilverlightGantt/TestPage.html">project Gantt representation done in Silverlight</a></strong> with complete capabilities for moving the timeline, etc.</p>  <p><a href="http://svdemo02.ilog.com/SilverlightGantt/TestPage.html"><img border="0" src="http://storage.timheuer.com/iloggantt.png" /></a></p>  <p>This example, created by iLog, demonstrates some creative uses of Silverlight for line of business applications.  Check out the other 27 new showcase entries on the site and subscribe to the feeds to stay up-to-date on what is happening on the Silverlight community.</p>  <div style="padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; float: none; padding-top: 0px" id="scid:0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:a98bb0e3-cff0-4989-8906-ac60a1a3be55" class="wlWriterEditableSmartContent"></div>
