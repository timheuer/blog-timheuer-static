<p>want to know how to use some of the <a href="http://dev.live.com/">windows live services</a>?  announced at the partner conference this week, some beta <a href="http://dev.live.com/QuickApps" rel="tag">windows live quickapps</a> have been released.  these provide out-of-box implementations for certain scenarios and take advantage of live services like expo, spaces, <a href="http://silverlight.live.com/" rel="tag">silverlight streaming</a>, virtual earth, etc.  </p> <p>and all source code is provided!  there are two of them available now, but more to come according to the site.  i downloaded the bike one, unzipped it and ran it right away...it was simple to set up.</p> <p><a title="contosobikes" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/66831573@N00/781149532/"><img alt="contosobikes" src="http://static.flickr.com/1432/781149532_18d3b8dae6.jpg" border="0" /></a></p> <p>check them <a href="http://dev.live.com/QuickApps">out here and download them at codeplex</a> (with the source).</p> <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="scid:0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:9f029b08-e3b8-4e63-960c-f4d84f1c89e1" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
