<p><img style="MARGIN: 0px 10px 0px 0px" height="146" width="120" align="left" alt="" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com/timheuer-img/petzoldgraceland.png" />well, kinda.  i just found out that if you live anywhere near memphis, tn that you might want to head over to the <a href="http://dayofdotnet.mnug.net/">memphis day of .net</a>.  charles petzold, the master, will be delivering the keynote.  he aims to <em>"explore the continuing love/hate relationship that both users and programmers have with graphics, and how WPF and Silverlight may change that relationship..."</em></p>
<p>aside from the keynote, <a href="http://www.charlespetzold.com/blog/2007/10/311018.html">petzold has indicated</a> that he'll be hanging out for the rest of the day to chat about WPF 3D, animation, or whatever.  if you are in the area, this sounds like a must-be-at event.  it is free, so you have no excuse not to be there on 10 NOV!</p>
