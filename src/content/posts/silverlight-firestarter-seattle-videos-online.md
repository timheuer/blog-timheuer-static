<p>at the end of november i had a chance to visit seattle to attend the silverlight firestarter event.  the entire day was captured on video.  those videos are now available at the <a href="http://visitmix.com/University/silverlight/firestarter/">visitmix.com site</a>.  you can stream them directly on the site or download them for offline viewing.  the sessions include content from <a rel="tag" href="http://silverlight.net/blogs/jesseliberty/">jesse liberty</a>, <a rel="tag" href="http://ux.artu.tv">arturo toledo</a>, <a rel="tag" href="http://blogs.msdn.com/webnext">laurence moroney</a>, <a rel="tag" href="http://www.adamkinney.com">adam kinney</a>, <a rel="tag" href="http://blogs.msdn.com/ebooth">ernie booth</a> and <a rel="tag" href="http://adamnathan.net">adam nathan</a>.</p>
<p>all good stuff.  <a href="http://visitmix.com/University/silverlight/firestarter/">enjoy</a>.</p>
