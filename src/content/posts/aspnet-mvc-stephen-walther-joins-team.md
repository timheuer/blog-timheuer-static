<p><img title="Stephen Walther" style="margin: 0px 10px 0px 0px" alt="Stephen Walther" src="http://s3.amazonaws.com:80/timheuer-img/stephenwalther.png" align="left" />Are you poking around with the latest <a href="http://www.asp.net/mvc">ASP.NET MVC</a> (Model-View-Controller) bits?  If so, hopefully you are already aware of the resources available to you and the fact that <strong><a href="http://haacked.com">Phil Haack</a></strong> is one of the program manager’s leading those efforts, and <strong><a href="http://www.hanselman.com/blog/">Scott Hanselman</a></strong> has been providing the community with some resources to jump start your learning.</p>  <p>Well, another member has just joined the ranks on the team I work for.  <strong>Stephen Walther</strong>, pictured here in his best book-cover pose ;-), has <a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/stephenwalther/archive/2008/06/05/i-m-now-working-at-microsoft.aspx">joined the team</a>.  He actually joined earlier this month, but I’m just now getting around to welcoming him to our team and checking out the stuff he’s been doing.  He’s been on a great clip lately and I’ve been reading his latest 11 tips on ASP.NET MVC on <a href="http://stephenwalther.com">his blog</a>.  I’m really glad to have him on our team as I think we’ve got some great resources for <a href="http://www.asp.net">ASP.NET</a>, <a href="http://www.asp.net/ajax">AJAX</a>, MVC, <a href="http://silverlight.net">Silverlight</a> and <a href="http://www.windowsclient.net">Windows Client</a>.</p>  <p>If you are messing around with the ASP.NET MVC framework, check out his site and subscribe.  We’ll be seeing a lot more of Stephen soon and I’m sure your ideas will be welcome to him as well!</p>  <div class="wlWriterSmartContent" id="scid:0767317B-992E-4b12-91E0-4F059A8CECA8:934c3a66-8616-4a88-b55b-be80a4f8e31c" style="padding-right: 0px; display: inline; padding-left: 0px; float: none; padding-bottom: 0px; margin: 0px; padding-top: 0px"></div>
