<p>the game, <a href="http://smcandrew.com/2008/02/silverlight_game-miniclip/">built by terralever</a>, falls into that 'casual gaming' category -- you know, the ones where you decide only to spend a few minutes taking a look and then end up forgetting that meeting so you can complete all the levels?  this one is an addicting game to figure out how to reveal all the levels possible.  check it out. </p>
<p> </p>
<p>you may remember the <strong>terralever</strong> name from the <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/archive/2007/06/26/zerogravity.aspx">zero gravity game</a>.  while that game was a proof to look at the capabilities of silverlight 1.1 at that time, the <strong>zombomatic</strong> game was built using silverlight 1.0 release technology so that it could go production.  congrats to the terralever team for this release and we look forward to seeing what you can do with silverlight 2 when available for production applications!</p>
