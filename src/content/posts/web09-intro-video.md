<p>In a few weeks I’ll be making the journey with <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/corrinab">Corrina</a> and <a href="http://ux.artu.tv">Arturo</a> from ‘the states’ to Auckland, New Zealand for the <a href="http://www.web09.org">WEB09 conference</a>.  I’m honored to be joining some great folks like Ryan Stewart, Dan Rubin, Jarred Bishop and more.  I’m looking forward to learning a lot from this conference and having discussions about user experience and RIA development.</p>
<p>The team at WEB09 released an introductory video for the conference and I must say, someone put a lot of effort into this video: <a href="http://vimeo.com/3916229?pg=embed&amp;sec=">WEB09 Intro Video</a></p>

<p>I’m very much looking forward to my time at WEB09 and the time in New Zealand in general (I’ve never been, so if you have some advice, please share).  Hopefully some of you will be there and we can meet in person and chat about <a href="http://silverlight.net/">Silverlight</a>! See you in a few weeks!</p>
