<p>I hope that you had a chance to download the <a href="http://preview.windows.com"><strong>Windows 8 Consumer Preview</strong></a>.  One of the things that you will absolutely want to do is watch Jensen’s video as a video walk-through of Windows 8 features, focusing on working with the operating system as a user, introducing the gestures and things you want to know about.  It is a great, short video and you should definitely watch it:</p>
<p><iframe style="height:288px;width:512px" src="http://channel9.msdn.com/Blogs/Windows-Blog/Windows-8-Consumer-Preview-Demo/player?w=512&amp;h=288" frameborder="0" scrolling="no"></iframe></p>
<p>As an avid keyboard user (and one without a touch device currently) I rely on the ‘desktop’ use of my Windows installation.  In addition to those things that Jensen mentions in his video, here’s a list of some helpful keyboard shortcuts…</p>
