<p>ahh, eula's -- you know the thing you click 'next' on to get your software installed? :-)  well, for silverlight, one little note may bite some tomorrow (or today for some of you) -- well, whatever, 01 AUG 2007.  that is when <strong><u>silverlight beta expires</u></strong> (and thus the initial alpha as well).  so even if you have the plugin and working apps, it won't work.</p>
<p>now is the time...update to the silverlight release candidate.  the release candidate also has an expiration, but now includes self-updating technology within the plugin.  <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/archive/2007/07/30/14166.aspx">check out this post</a> to ensure you get the latest sdk's and bits to get your samples up and running for your visitors!</p>
