design.  You may look at this site here and say it doesn’t, and that is okay…I’m trying my best :-).</p>  <p><a href="http://www.smashingmagazine.com"><img style="border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; amazing sites for inspiration and design resources that I’ve added to my toolbox.  Of all the feeds I subscribe to, I can count on the syndicated content coming from them to be 99.999% inspiring and
