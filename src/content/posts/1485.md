<P>&#8220;<EM>I'll put grape kool-aid in the drinking fountains...&#8221;</EM></P>
<P>that was my campaign platform when running for school government president at elementary school.</P>
<P>no matter what side of the political fence you live on, you have to look at these debates and candidates with a grain of salt.&nbsp; would i have ever been able to put kool-aid in the drinking fountains...probably not.&nbsp; as an individual it would have been very difficult, if not nearly impossible.&nbsp; however, with a strong voice (in my case other 12-13 year olds) behind me, it might have been possible.&nbsp; at that is the important point in my opinion...who can you stand behind to support their vision?</P>
<P>the debates last night were helpful to me to see both candidates in front of each other.&nbsp; i love a good verbal lashing and thought there was plenty of that.&nbsp; i hope that you are registered to vote and voice your opinion in november.&nbsp; if you aren't, shame on you...seriously, shame on you.&nbsp; there is no reason why every person shouldn't exercise your voice and not let a single person control what you want.</P>
<P>i encourage you to look within yourselves, your lives and for the good of the country...and decide who you believe will be the best leader behind other powers of our government.</P>
<P>i watched the debates with a slight chuckle...as each said &#8220;i promise...i will...i can...&#8221; -- all the time thinking to myself...<EM>mmm....grape kool-aid</EM></P>
