<p>I’m a little late to the game posting information about it, but there is a plethora of <a href="http://silverlight.net"><strong>Silverlight</strong></a> stuff to do this week in the Seattle/Redmond area this week.  Sleepless in Seattle…definitely not – Silverlight in Seattle baby!</p>  <p><img style="display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto" src="http://adamkinney.com/images/blog/slweek.gif" /></p>  <p>Allow me to interpret <a href="http://www.adamkinney.com">Adam</a>’s vector goods…</p>  <ul>   <li>14 Sep (tonight!): Jesse Liberty is <a href="http://www.dotnetda.com/Events/EventNewsletter.aspx?EventDate=9/14/2009">presenting at the .NET DA user group meeting</a> on .NET RIA Services.</li>    <li>15 Sep: <a href="http://www.nerddinner.com/959">Nerd Dinner</a>!!!  Just a casual gathering of people who speak in acronyms and like food.  Bellevue Crossroads food court (awesome choices for anyone).</li>    <li>16 Sep: I’ll be presenting (thanks to <a href="http://www.hackingsilverlight.net">David</a> for the invite!) to the <a href="http://www.seattled2ig.org/?p=239">Seattle Interact</a> group on styling/templating interfaces for Silverlight as well as some on behaviors.</li>    <li>17 Sep: <a href="http://msevents.microsoft.com/CUI/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=1032422412&amp;Culture=en-US">Seattle Firestarter</a> event.  <a href="http://weblogs.asp.net/scottgu">ScottGu</a> and friends.  I’ll be doing my best to demonstrate some of the key scenarios for Silverlight and hope to inspire your thoughts.</li> </ul>  <p>With the exception of tonight, I’ll be at each of these events and am looking forward to spending the week in sunny Seattle with you folks.  Be sure to stop by and introduce yourself!</p>  <div style="padding-bottom: 0px;
