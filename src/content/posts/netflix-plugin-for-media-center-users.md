<p>Are you a <strong>Netflix</strong> customer and have a <strong>Media Center</strong> PC?  Check out what one of our Media Center MVPs re-birthed.  <strong>Anthony Park</strong>, a Media Center <strong>MVP</strong>, has regenerated a project from back in 2004 (<a href="http://www.unmitigatedrisk.com/archive/2008/02/10/174.aspx">originally developed by Ryan Hurst</a>) and released <a href="http://www.anpark.com/index.php/2008/02/26/new-release-mynetflix-v21-beta/">MyNetflix v2.1</a>.</p>
<p>I'm not a Media Center nor Netflix user but the user interface is impressive and done very well to look like a part of the overall Media Center experience.  Here's a view of your Netflix queue:</p>
<p><img height="359" width="640" alt="" src="http://www.anpark.com/uploaded_images/MyNetflixSmall_Queue_20080226.jpg" /></p>
<p>You can <a href="http://www.anpark.com/index.php/2008/02/26/new-release-mynetflix-v21-beta/">see other screenshots</a> of browsing 'top' categories, movie details and he's also now incorporated 'watch now' information as well.</p>
<p>Great work on extending and keeping <strong>MyNetflix</strong> alive Anthony! </p>
