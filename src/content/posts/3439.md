<p>more info on the webui 3 release: <a href="http://www.componentart.com/whatsnew.aspx#news26">http://www.componentart.com/whatsnew.aspx#news26</a></p>
<p>view live demo: <a href="http://webui30.componentart.com/">http://webui30.componentart.com</a></p>
