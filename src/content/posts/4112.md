<p>i&rsquo;m traveling again&hellip;but as i continue to travel i notice things a lot more&hellip;especially in airports.</p>
<p>today at sky harbor, i waited in the security line as they asked me to ensure my belt buckle was removed and my shoes removed.&nbsp; as i&rsquo;m doing this i notice all airline employees and pilots able to pass through freely, not being required to do the same&hellip;and they have shoes (note: i travel wearing my birkenstocks now in hopes i don&rsquo;t have to take them off &mdash; hasn&rsquo;t happened yet).&nbsp; i felt a little odd that certain people get special treatment.&nbsp; shouldn&rsquo;t security be security?</p>
<p>then while i&rsquo;m waiting at the gate to board the plane i notice a lot of standby passengers getting on this flight (phoenix to las vegas).&nbsp; one woman got her ticket, then the gate attendant came around the counter and looked at the passengers shoes &mdash; then she called a supervisor over to look at them (note: the woman was wearing teva sandals).&nbsp; i thought this was odd.&nbsp; i then learned it was an airline employee flying standby&hellip;they had rules on what they could wear when they fly on the airline&hellip;huh, i thought&hellip;</p>
<p>this time i was prepared for my travel&hellip;got the linksys travel router for my trips now&hellip;easier than relying on any wireless network existing.&nbsp; it took a few steps to get the standard hotel accept function working and a few hacks/mac clones&hellip;but it worked&hellip;highly recommended.&nbsp; that combined with my igo power adapter make great travel companions.</p>
