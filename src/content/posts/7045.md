<P>in case you are a microsoft developer and have been under a rock today...</P>
<P>Microsoft announced Go Live licenses this morning for WCF (Windows Communication Foundation / &#8220;Indigo&#8221;) and WF (Windows Workflow Foundation), which allow customers to use the January Go Live releases of WCF and WF in their deployment environments. (Note that these are unsupported Go Lives.) </P>
<P>More information about the Go Live program is at <A href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/winfx/getthebeta/golive/default.aspx">http://msdn.microsoft.com/winfx/getthebeta/golive/default.aspx</A>. </P>
<P>There are also WCF and WF community sites, at: <BR><A href="http://windowscommunication.net">http://windowscommunication.net</A> <BR><A href="http://windowsworkflow.net">http://windowsworkflow.net</A> </P>
<P>The community sites are intended to give users everything needed to start using WCF and WF today. </P>
