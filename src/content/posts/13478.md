<p>well, i decided to play around a bit with the new <a href="http://timheuer.com/blog/archive/2006/09/19/13475.aspx">visual studio tools for office second edition that was released today</a>.  a while back (actually at <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/korbyp/archive/2005/10/11/479889.aspx" rel="tag">codeslam</a> before the microsoft mvp summit), i was talking with richard <a title="Richard keep thinking that <a vsto tools.  it was amazingly simple to do and implement a custom ribbon ui add-in:</p> <p><img alt="SnopeIt1" src="http://static.flickr.com/94/247862089_c71d394925.jpg" border="0" /></p> <p>so i
