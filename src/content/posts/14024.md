<p>a colleague recently showed me the latest edition of wired magazine (something i used to subscribe to but don't anymore just because i'm more "online").  after getting past the cover, peter showed me <a href="http://www.wired.com/wired/archive/15.04/wired40_microsoft.html">the article</a> where <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/" rel="tag">channel 9</a> was featured and <a href="http://www.jeffsandquist.com/" rel="tag">jeff sandquist</a> interviewed.</p> <p>first, i think that is pretty rewarding for the <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/" rel="tag">channel 9</a> team [for the record i'm not on that team...i wonder if they have an opening for field reporters :-)].  i know that jeff and <a href="http://www.neopoleon.com/">others</a> <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/Niners/Charles">team</a> <a href="http://adamkinney.com/">members</a> work hard on channel 9 and reaching out to the community, bringing transparency to microsoft, etc.</p> <blockquote> <p><strong>SIDEBAR: What is Channel 9?</strong><br />You can take a look on <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/" rel="tag">channel9.msdn.com</a> for what it is and how it is evolving, but here it is in a nutshell (from my words).  Ever fly on United airlines?  Ever plug in your headphones and listen to the airplane audio?  If not, do it and turn to channel 9 -- you'll hear the cockpit, conversations with air traffic control, etc...a view from the inside.  There's the genesis...bringing you a cockpit view from Microsoft...and thus <a href="http://channel9.msdn.com/" rel="tag">Channel 9</a> for Microsoft.</p> <p>This has spawned other communities for a "prosumer" crowd (read: probably not as propellerheady as you/me) called <a href="http://www.on10.net/" rel="tag">On10</a>.  This has some great stories/content as well that you might want to look at.<br /></p></blockquote> <p>i thought one of the funniest things was the photographs in the article (by the way, wired has the article online if you aren't a subscriber).  i particularly liked this one:</p> <p><img height="240" alt="Jeff Sandquist"
