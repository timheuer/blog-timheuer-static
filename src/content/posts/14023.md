<p>i'm on a plane back from seattle and the movie on the plane is the latest rocky balboa.  now unfortunately my wife and i have already seen this movie and i'm now reminded of the horrible nature of it.  i'm watching the scene in the beginning where rocky goes to his old bar and meets up again with "little marie" again.</p> <p>since when did rocky become an *incredible* idiot?  when little marie tells him she's sorry for the loss of his wife, his reply: "yeah, woman cancer" -- wtf kind of a dialogue is that -- seriously, does anyone (even people with their heads rocked) talk like that?  other terrible lines? "jamaica huh, eurpoean."  ugh, i don't even want to waste any more of your time.</p> <p>don't see this movie.</p>
