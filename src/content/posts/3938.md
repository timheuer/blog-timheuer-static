<p>so i&rsquo;m waiting in a customer lobby (which happens to actually be the human resources department) and waiting for my appointment &mdash; i&rsquo;m way early &mdash; one of those &ldquo;haven&rsquo;t been there, not sure how long it will take to drive from the sticks where i live to civilization&rdquo; situations.</p>
<p>there was a guy here waiting in the lobby as well, then suddenly gets up and starts working behind the desk with the receptionist.&nbsp; he&rsquo;s an older guy&hellip;i have no idea what he&rsquo;s doing, but it sounds like the receptionist just gave him some menial tasks to do&hellip;then i heard this:</p>
<p>receptionist: &ldquo;so i hear you&rsquo;re an author or something&rdquo;</p>
<p>guy: &ldquo;well, yeah kinda&hellip;i&rsquo;m actually pretty famous in what i do&rdquo;</p>
<p>receptionist: &ldquo;really?&rdquo;</p>
<p>guy: &ldquo;yeah, i&rsquo;m famous for collecting fountain pens.&rdquo;</p>
<p><em>my ears now are more finely tuned in&hellip;after all, how often do you hear about someone famous for fountain pens?</em></p>
<p>guy (without pause, not giving the receptionist any chance to back out now): &ldquo;it&rsquo;s actually quite interesting, this history of the pall boint pen when you think about it.&rdquo;</p>
<p><em>at this point, i&rsquo;m wondering if the guy can hear me laughing&hellip;</em></p>
<p>guy: &ldquo;there&rsquo;s been a lot of amazing technology and design put in to ball point pens and fount&hellip;&rdquo;</p>
<p><em>rrrring&hellip;in true &ldquo;saved by the bell&rdquo; fashion, the reception was let off the hook</em></p>
<p>lucky for me, i was able to get into a meeting, but i can&rsquo;t imagine the how the conversation progressed&hellip;i can only imagine, the receptionist will never look at her bic pen the same any more&hellip;</p>
